On this all expences paid vacation to outer space we will visit the sights of the solar system!

Hold on tight as we take you through the Asteroid Belt located between Mars and Jupiter. Don't worry, the asteroids are more spread out than you may think, and there will be plenty of opportunity to rock climb on them!

Let's check out the brightest planet in the night sky! Also known as the Earth's twin, Venus has spectacular cloud coverage that never goes away and never dissapoints.

Make a wish as we journey to the Kuiper Belt, home of icey dwarf planets and comets. Perfect time to use our available spaceboards!
